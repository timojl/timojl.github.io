Hi there! I am a research group leader in the lab of Alexander Ecker at University of Göttingen. My research focuses on scalable, multi-modal machine learning methods. Particularly, I am interested in techniques for sample-efficient and unsupervised learning as well as incorporating user interaction.

Short Bio

Before, I was a postdoc working with Alexander Ecker. I did my PhD in 2019 with Florentin Wörgötter working on robotic perception at the University of Göttingen. Before that, I received Bachelor and Master from the Faculty of Computer Science at the University of Magdeburg with an emphasis on medical image processing.
