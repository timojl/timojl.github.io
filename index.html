Hi there! I am currently a postdoc working with Alexander Ecker at University of Göttingen. My research focuses on applying machine learning for perceiving, understanding and representing dynamic scenes. Particularly, I am interested in methods for sample-efficient and unsupervised learning.

Short Bio

I did my PhD with Florentin Wörgötter working on robotic perception at the University of Göttingen. Before that, I received Bachelor and Master from the Faculty of Computer Science at the University of Magdeburg with an emphasis on medical image processing. I spend an Erasmus semester at the University of Uppsala in Sweden.
